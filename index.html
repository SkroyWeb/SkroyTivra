About Meal Mitra

We're building bridges between surplus and scarcity, transforming food waste into nourishment for those who need it most.

The Problem

  • Every year, approximately 1.3 billion tons of food is wasted globally, while over 800 million people go to bed hungry.
  • 50% of all food waste happens in households and restaurants, much of which is still perfectly edible.
  • Food waste contributes to 8-10% of global greenhouse gas emissions.
  • Despite abundance on one end, 1 in 9 people suffer from chronic undernourishment.

Our Solution

  • A decentralized platform that bridges the gap between food donors, NGOs, and volunteers.
  • Real-time matching algorithm that connects surplus food with nearby distribution centers.
  • Volunteer network for efficient and quick food pickup and delivery.
  • Digital tracking system allowing donors to see the impact of their contributions.

Our Mission

To create a seamless, tech-powered food-sharing network that ensures surplus food reaches those in need, reducing waste and fighting hunger simultaneously. We aim to build a community-driven ecosystem where sharing becomes the norm, not the exception.

Our Vision

A zero-food-waste society where hunger is a thing of the past. We envision a world where resources are shared efficiently, communities are connected through compassion, and every bit of surplus is transformed into sustenance for someone in need.
